In Jen's own words

What help do people come to you for?

"Usually design critique and research strategy. People bring me half-formed ideas or work that isn't landing and want to understand why. I'm good at diagnosing root causes rather than just describing symptoms — which is the part that's actually hard."

What problems do you love solving?

"The ones where the product problem and the people problem are the same problem. When you trace a confusing UX back to a fuzzy team decision, or a high churn rate back to misaligned priorities — that's where I want to spend my time. I really love diving into these type of issues with engineers and PMs and working together as a team to solve it. It may not always be glamorous, but it's where the real impact is made."

What does a great work day look like?

"A morning doing actual design work with no interruptions. A long lunch walk. An afternoon of reviews and conversation where I feel like I'm genuinely useful rather than just present. Finishing the day with something tangible to show for it."
