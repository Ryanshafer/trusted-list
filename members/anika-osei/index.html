In Anika's own words

What help do people come to you for?

"Making sense of complexity. When a product or a team is stuck and nobody can quite name why, I help surface the underlying structure. Whether that's a service blueprint, a systems map, or just a conversation that cuts through the noise."

What problems do you love solving?

"The ones that turn out to be several simpler problems wearing a coat. Most 'wicked' problems in organisations are actually a cluster of easier problems nobody's had the courage or the space to disentangle. That's where I want to be."

What does a great work day look like?

"Thinking hard in the morning. Teaching or sharing in the afternoon. A walk somewhere green in between. Ending the day with something I didn't understand when I started."
