In Sam's own words

What help do people come to you for?

"Usually roadmap decisions and stakeholder dynamics. People come to me when they're stuck between competing priorities or need someone to pressure-test their thinking before a big conversation. I've made most of the classic PM mistakes, so I'm good at spotting the traps before you fall in them."

What problems do you love solving?

"The ones where the real blocker isn't the product — it's the room. Misaligned incentives, competing definitions of success, teams that are rowing in different directions. Getting clarity there unlocks everything else."

What does a great work day look like?

"A morning of deep thinking with no Slack. A focused spec review with the team where we actually disagree productively. A short call where I help someone think through a problem. Ending the day knowing which three things matter most for tomorrow."
